Our Approach

Asoko collects and constantly updates company information through a variety of frontline data acquisition channels. Before publication, all data goes through a quality assurance process consisting of in-person verification and automated checks. Updates are done on a monthly basis.

Technology

Supported by the latest know-how in data science, we turn raw data into actionable information. New data-driven insights and analytics allow for superior research on African companies.

In-country sources

Our local research analysts, based out of three regional offices, are working with executives and data partners to monitor corporate data from key companies in the region. As we consolidate our footprint in the largest economies, we are also expanding data collection operations to surrounding countries across the continent.

Clients

Our clients include investors, traders, global corporates and African companies looking to identify deals, conduct due diligence, generate commercial leads or report on the region’s dynamic business environment.
